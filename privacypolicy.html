Privacy Policy for Spendlite App

Last updated: December 2025

Spendlite (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use Spendlite.

1. No Personal Data Collection

Spendlite is a fully offline app. All your spending records, transactions, and personal data are stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share your information.

2. Local Storage Only

All app data is saved directly on your device. No internet connection is required, and no information is sent to our servers or any third-party service.

3. Data Security

Your data is private and stays on your device. We recommend backing up your device if you want to preserve your records, as we have no access to recover lost data.

4. No Third-Party Tracking

We do not use analytics, advertising, or any third-party tracking in the app. Your usage is completely private.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy, please contact us at: ayomidepeat@gmail.com
